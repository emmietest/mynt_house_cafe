Fun Facts

Discover Morocco

The Blue City

In Morocco’s Rif Mountains lies Chefchaouen — a dreamy town painted in endless shades of blue. The color symbolizes peace, spirituality, and the boundless sky.

Learn More

First to Recognize U.S. Independence

Morocco became the first nation to recognize the United States in 1777 — beginning one of the world’s oldest friendships built on respect and shared values.

Learn More

Oldest University
in Morocco

Founded in 859 AD by Fatima al-Fihri in Fez, Al-Qarawiyyin is recognized as the world’s oldest continuously operating university — a beacon of knowledge and culture.
