About Mynt House Café

Roots & Inspiration

Our story begins thousands of miles away, in the lively, sun-soaked streets of Morocco.

There, coffee and tea are more than drinks — they’re a daily ritual of connection, conversation, and warmth. The spice markets of Marrakech, the elegance of Fez, and the breezy charm of Essaouira all shaped our love for café culture and the sense of belonging it inspires.

From those fragrances and traditions, an idea began to take shape: to create a space where the essence of Moroccan café life — its aromas, artistry, and hospitality — could be experienced in a fresh, modern way. Our dream was to carry this rich cultural tapestry across oceans and share it with people who value community and meaningful connection.

Finding Home in Ann Arbor

When it came time to plant roots abroad, Ann Arbor, Michigan felt like a natural choice.

Known for its welcoming spirit, vibrant energy, and love of culture, Ann Arbor mirrors the very heartbeat of Morocco. Its curiosity and openness reminded us of the neighborhoods where we grew up — places where stories were shared over mint tea and the air was always filled with laughter.

Here, we found not just a city, but a home.

The Space & The Experience

AWith our hearts rooted in Morocco and our eyes set on Ann Arbor, we poured every detail of our heritage into creating a welcoming café.

From the warm tones inspired by desert sunsets to handcrafted lanterns reminiscent of Fez medinas, every corner tells a story. Our menu celebrates authentic Moroccan flavors — bold coffee blends, spiced teas, and pastries layered with history — while weaving in local twists inspired by Michigan’s seasons.

More than just décor and taste, Mynt House is about atmosphere. It’s a place where conversations linger, students gather, families pause to reconnect, and travelers find a slice of home. The soft rhythms of Marrakech fill the air, while the scent of cardamom and cinnamon welcomes you the moment you step inside.

Bridging Two Worlds

And of course, there’s the University of Michigan — a crossroads of students, scholars, and dreamers.

The bold Michigan blue, a color that symbolizes pride and tradition here, resonates beautifully with Morocco’s iconic blue — from the painted alleys of Chefchaouen to the intricate tilework of our heritage. That shared hue connects our two worlds — both steeped in history, creativity, and the pursuit of something greater.

What Mynt House Represents

  • Mynt House is more than a café — it’s a cultural meeting point.
  • A taste of Morocco in the heart of Michigan.
  • A place where flavors, stories, and people come together — and where every cup celebrates the beauty of connection.

Experience the spirit of Morocco at Mynt House Café — where authentic flavors, rich traditions, and warm ambiance come together for a true cultural escape.
